Bloody Mary

Description:

Tired of drinking the same old bloody Mary? Try this classic with a difference – the addition of sherry adds a subtle sweetness and richness to the tomato juice that you’ll love.

Ingredients:

  • 200ml chilled tomato juice
  • 50ml chilled vodka
  • 1 tsp dry sherry
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 3 dashes of Worcestershire sauce
  • 3 dashes of Tabasco sauce
  • A good grind of black pepper
  • celery stick
  • green olives
  • lemon wedge

Steps:

  1. In a jug, mix together 200ml chilled tomato juice, 50ml chilled vodka, 1 tsp, 1 tsp dry sherry, 1 tsp lemon juice and 3 dashes each Worcestershire and Tabasco sauces.
  2. Season with a good grind of black pepper, adding extra Worcestershire for sweetness or Tabasco for heat, if you like.
  3. Fill a tall glass with ice and pour over the tomato mixture. Garnish with a celery stick, green olives and a lemon wedge to serve (optional).
